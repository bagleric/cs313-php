Eric Bagley

My name as you now know is Eric Bagley. I was born and raised on a small ranch and dairy farm in victor Idaho. Thats why my wife calls me her farmboy. Despite growing up on a farm I have always been interested in computers. That interest did not lead me to coding until just a few years ago. apart from that, I love both listening and playing music, being in the outdoors, and spending time with family.

We decided to build our friendship that turned into a lifelong relationship. We were married January 2017 in the Salt lake temple. She is a music major and plays the violin. Currently, we are finishing up school. I will graduate in December of 2017 and Amira, my wife, will graduate winter semester of 2018.

Our plans as of right now are undecided. We are still analyzing our options. We do hop to have a large family, live long and healthy lives, and eventually serve missions for the Church.
